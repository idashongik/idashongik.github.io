Strategies for Advancing the Design of Mobile Grocery Services in the Conventional Market

Jooyeon Lee · 이주연
Study Abstract

This service expects that the development of online traditional market shopping service which has not frequent use also activating traditional market and relevant area. Therefore, we research the environment and usability of current online traditional market and find solutions to suggest how the mobile traditional market shopping service has to be developed. We hope the traditional market can take back a lively and vibrant atmosphere again.
